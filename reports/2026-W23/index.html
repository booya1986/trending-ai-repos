למה זה בטרנד

It is a clean example of the "AI for science" wave: rather than fine-tuning, it reshapes the data representation so general LLMs reason about chemistry well, and packaging it as an agent skill made it easy to try.

דוגמת שימוש

A neutral domain example outside your core work: a chemistry researcher uses it to have an LLM propose and edit candidate molecules conversationally. The transferable lesson for you is the core idea: changing how you represent a domain to the model often beats fine-tuning.

למה זה רלוונטי לך

Not directly applicable, but it is a strong illustration of representation-over-fine-tuning, a principle worth carrying into how you structure knowledge for AI in your own projects.
